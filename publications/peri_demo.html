Demo: Adaptive Time-Frequency Scattering for Periodic Modulation Recognition in Music Signals

Contributions:

  • Representation: adaptive time-frequency scattering transform for representing playing techniques
  • Application: a general framework for recognising periodic modulations
  • Dataset: an annotated dataset of Chinese bamboo flute performances
