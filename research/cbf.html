Chinese Bamboo Flute

    Chinese bamboo flute, also known as the Dizi or Zhudi, is one of the world’s most ancient instruments, with a large repertoire of playing techniques. The acts of breathing, fingering, and tonguing, in interaction with a thin membrane glued to the CBF’s resonance hole, create unique sounds and subtle sound nuances in performance, providing a rich platform of analysing playing techniques.
