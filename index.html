I am a postdoc researcher of the French National Centre for Scientific Research (CNRS) based at the Nantes Laboratory of Digital Sciences (LS2N), working with Vincent Lostanlen and Mathieu Lagrange. I obtained my PhD degree in 2021 from the Centre for Digital Music (C4DM), Queen Mary University of London, supervised by Dr. Emmanouil Benetos and Prof. Elaine Chew. My research focus is explainable audio models, which aims at opening the blackbox of deep learning models for audio signals.
