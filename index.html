I am a postdoc researcher of the French National Centre for Scientific Research (CNRS) based at the Nantes Laboratory of Digital Sciences (LS2N), working with Dr. Vincent Lostanlen. I obtained my PhD degree in 2021 from the Centre for Digital Music (C4DM), Queen Mary University of London, supervised by Dr. Emmanouil Benetos and Prof. Elaine Chew. My research focus is explainable computational models for sounds in general such as music, bio-acoustic signals, and anomalous sounds.
